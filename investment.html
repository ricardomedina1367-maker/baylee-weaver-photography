The things people ask

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 25% retainer holds your date. The remaining balance is due one week before your session. Both are handled simply through the Honeybook client portal.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens. You receive one complimentary reschedule within 60 days, no questions asked. Just reach out as soon as you know and we'll find a new date that works.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! You can pay in installments through the Honeybook client portal. Split your balance into 2 or 3 payments — whatever works best for your budget.
