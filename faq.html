Before You Book

Baylee is based in Plano and serves the greater DFW area — including Frisco, Allen, McKinney, Richardson, Garland, and beyond. Studio sessions are available in her Plano home studio. Outdoor sessions can take place at locations of your choice or at one of her curated spots.

Newborn sessions should be booked by the end of your second trimester (around 28–30 weeks). Maternity sessions are best at 28–34 weeks. Family sessions book 4–6 weeks in advance. Peak seasons (fall, spring) fill up fast.

Sessions & What to Expect

Newborn sessions are 2–3 hours (babies set the pace). Maternity sessions are 60 minutes. Family sessions are 45 minutes.

You'll receive a full styling guide after booking. In general: soft neutrals, muted tones, and flowy fabrics photograph beautifully. Avoid logos, neon, or heavy patterns. Baylee can help coordinate your whole family's outfits.

Newborn: Your home or Baylee's Plano studio. Maternity: Outdoor golden-hour locations or studio. Family: Parks, your backyard, or curated outdoor spots. Baylee has a list of favorite DFW locations she can share.
