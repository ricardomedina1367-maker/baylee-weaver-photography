The Story

How it all began

Baylee grew up in the DFW area and discovered her love for photography while documenting her sister's pregnancy. What started as a borrowed camera and a curiosity became an obsession — and eventually, a calling.

She studied visual arts and spent years second-shooting for established photographers before launching her own studio in 2023. Those years taught her the discipline of craft, the patience of a long session, and the irreplaceable instinct to know when to press the shutter.

She specializes in the quiet, unhurried moments — the way a newborn curls into a chest, the way a partner looks at a bump, the chaos of a golden-hour family session. The image nobody planned for.

Baylee shoots primarily in natural light, in-home and at curated outdoor locations across Plano, Frisco, Allen, and McKinney. Her studio is calm, warm, and specifically designed for tiny humans.

The Approach

What I believe matters

01

Less posing, more presence

I give gentle guidance but never stiff direction. The best shots happen between poses — in the exhale, the laugh, the look. I'm here to capture who you actually are, not a version of you that feels uncomfortable.

02

Light over everything

I plan sessions around golden hour and window light. No harsh flash, ever. Natural light is the most flattering, most timeless medium — and it's the foundation of every image I make.

03

You'll love these photos

I won't deliver a gallery I'm not proud of. Quality over quantity, always. You'll receive fewer, better images — each one something you'd actually want to hang on a wall or print for a grandparent.
